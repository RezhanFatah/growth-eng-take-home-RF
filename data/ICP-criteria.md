# Who to Target (ICP Criteria)

## Platform & Tech
- **Shopify or Shopify Plus** - Important
- Bonus: Gorgias or Zendesk in stack

## Traffic & Scale
- **300k+ monthly visits** (450k+ ideal)
- \$10M+ revenue

## Who to Reach Out To
- Director / VP / Manager of eCommerce (conversions)
- Head of DTC
- Director of Customer Support (automating tickets / saving time)
- Head of Customer Experience
- Sometimes CMO (converting their earned traffic)

## Intent Signals (Who is in the market for Remark?)
- Site has no live chat or is running slow / poor chat experience
- Hiring for eComm roles (bonus if mentioning "conversion rate" in job post). Sumble is a great free tool for tracking this
- Competitors already on Remark
- Spending on paid ads (you're getting targeted by their Instagram Ads). Shows they're paying for traffic, they'll want to convert those visitors
- Recent Shopify migration
